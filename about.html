About Us

Central Pathology is committed to delivering accurate, reliable, and timely diagnostic services while adhering to the highest standards of medical excellence and ethical practice.


The laboratory is led by Dr. Anita Garg (M.B.B.S., M.D.), a highly experienced pathologist with over 30 years of expertise in Pathology and Microbiology. She has received advanced training in Radiation and Isotope Applications (R.I.A.) from B.A.R.C., Mumbai, and possesses specialized knowledge in Genetics. Her vast experience and dedication ensure precise diagnostics and patient-focused care.
