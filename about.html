About Us

The laboratory is led by Dr. Anita Garg (M.B.B.S., M.D.), a senior pathologist with over 30 years of experience in Pathology and Microbiology. She has undergone advanced training in Radiation and Isotope Applications (R.I.A.) from B.A.R.C., Mumbai and holds specialized expertise in Genetics.

Dr. Garg’s diagnostic expertise spans hematology, clinical pathology, histopathology, cytology, microbiology, and molecular diagnostics. Her meticulous approach and deep clinical insight ensure that every report issued is both scientifically accurate and clinically meaningful.


With decades of consistent performance, Central Pathology has earned long-standing trust in the medical community. The laboratory has supported thousands of accurate diagnoses through dependable reporting, ethical operations, and continuous upgradation of diagnostic methodologies.

Our services encompass routine and specialized investigations including preventive health checkups, disease monitoring, advanced pathology testing, and clinician-supported diagnostic interpretation. Continuous training, technology adoption, and stringent internal audits enable us to maintain high standards of quality and reliability.

The laboratory’s commitment to excellence, transparency, and patient welfare has made it a preferred diagnostic center for generations of patients and healthcare professionals.
